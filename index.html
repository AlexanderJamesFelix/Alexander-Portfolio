2D animation

the process of creating the illusion of movement in a two-dimensional space. This is achieved by creating a series of individual drawings or images, called frames, and playing them in rapid succession.

3d animation

the process of creating moving images in a three-dimensional (3D) environment, giving the illusion that these digital objects are moving through a 3D space.

character design

the process of creating and defining the visual appearance, personality, behavior, and aesthetics of a character, usually for use in films, games, animation, illustration, or comics.

Visual effects

the process of creating or manipulating imagery outside the context of a live-action shot in filmmaking and video production.
